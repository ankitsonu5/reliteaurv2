The Origin Story:
A Kitchen Revolution

When our founder Raguram Raghulal embraced a gluten free lifestyle,
he didn't settle — he innovated. Like every gluten free food seeker, he found himself asking the same questions: "Will I have to compromise on taste?
Is it right for my palate? Will I miss out on delicious food?"

He turned this concern and empathy into a tenacious drive towards making gluten free cuisines desirable as any other loved tasty fare.

Armed with decades of baking wizardry and an obsession with perfection,
we set out to prove that gluten free ingredients can indeed help you whip up the tastiest dishes. No love lost: only your taste buds loving every bite.

Today, we're rewriting the rules of what gluten free can be.
One delectable bite at a time.

Why AOECS Makes All the Difference

The Gold Standard of Gluten Free:

Imagine if there was a certification so rigorous, so internationally respected, that celiacs worldwide trusted it with their lives. That's AOECS.

What Makes AOECS Special:

  • 39 Celiac Societies across Europe, and AOECS affiliate members
    across the globe
  • Annual Facility Audits: No shortcuts, no compromises
  • ≤20ppm Gluten Guarantee: Scientifically validated reliability
  • 23,000+ Licensed Gluten Free Products: Worldwide trust network
  • The Crossed Grain Symbols: Recognised globally

In India, we're practically the final word in gluten free.
